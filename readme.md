# 🐱 Hacker Cat - Desktop Chaos Pet

A mischievous hacker cat that lives on your desktop, follows your mouse, and causes hilarious chaos!

## ✨ Features

- 👀 **Eyes follow your mouse cursor** - creepy but cute!
- 🐭 **Cat follows your mouse** around the screen like a real pet
- 😾 **Rage meter** - fills when you click the cat
- 💥 **100% rage = CHAOS MODE** - fake popups, terminals, matrix effects!
- 💤 **Goes to sleep** when inactive
- 🖱️ **Drag to move** anywhere on screen
- ⚙️ **Configurable settings** - mouse follow speed, rage system, auto-startup
